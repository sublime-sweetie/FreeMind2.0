Here's some of our Frequently Asked Questions

What is the health tracker?

The health tracker will provide the user a set of questions asking about how they feel. These questions will range from “How are you feeling today” to “When was your last meal”. The goal with this health tracker is to provide the user a space to express themselves

Is all my information private?

No information is shared or seen by anyone else but you! This is a resource we created specifically for your benefit!

How long does it take to create an account?

It only takes 2 minutes to create an account!

How do I see a list of resources near me?

With an account you will have access to a map that lists tons of different resources that are available to you!

Why is mental health important?

Mental health is important because it affects everything. It affects the way you adapt, solve problems, your mood, productivity, etc.

How will I know if my insurance can cover this?

Our site will provide you with the location and phone numbers to a plethora of resources near you. You have the choice to checkbox any specific resource that you are looking for.
